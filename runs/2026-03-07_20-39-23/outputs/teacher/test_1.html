About Meridian National Bank

Founded in 1952, Meridian National Bank has served communities across the Northeast for over seven decades. What began as a single branch built on the principles of trust, integrity, and personal service has grown into a premier regional institution with 12 locations.

Under the leadership of CEO Robert Chambers, we remain committed to the values that have guided us from the beginning — putting our customers first, investing in our communities, and delivering financial solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

As an FDIC-insured institution, your deposits are protected, and your trust is our most valued asset.
