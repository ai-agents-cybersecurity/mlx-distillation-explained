Our Story

Harvest & Vine was born from a simple belief: the best meals begin in rich soil. Nestled among the rolling vineyards of Napa Valley, our restaurant celebrates the profound connection between land, season, and table.

Under the direction of Chef Isabella Torres, a James Beard Award nominee, our kitchen transforms estate-grown produce, sustainably raised proteins, and foraged ingredients into dishes that honor both American culinary tradition and the sun-soaked flavors of the Mediterranean.

Every evening, our menu tells the story of what's growing, what's ripe, and what the valley has to offer — right now, at this very moment.

"Cooking is an act of listening — to the soil, to the season, to the ingredient itself. My job is simply to not get in the way."

— Chef Isabella Torres
