About Harvest & Vine

Located in the heart of Napa Valley, California

Harvest & Vine is a farm-to-table restaurant that celebrates the beauty of seasonal American cuisine with Mediterranean influences. Our chef, Isabella Torres, has been nominated for a James Beard award and brings her passion for local, sustainable ingredients to every dish.

With a warm, earthy tone interior and a patio seating area overlooking the vineyards, Harvest & Vine is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a meal with friends and family.
